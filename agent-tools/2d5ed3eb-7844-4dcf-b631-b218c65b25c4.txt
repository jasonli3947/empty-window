# Damage

 

[Ash's Pokémon](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Category:Ash%27s_Pok%C3%A9mon) injured after a battle

Damage (Japanese: ダメージ damage) is a loss of a Pokémon's [HP](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/HP) that happens as the result of a [physical](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Physical_move) or [special](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Special_move) [attack](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Move) used against it by another Pokémon.

## Damage calculation

| This section is incomplete.Please feel free to edit this section to add missing information and complete it. Reason: Anything that may have potentially been missed or inaccurate |
| --- |

Except for [moves that deal direct damage](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Category:Moves_that_deal_direct_damage), the damage dealt when a Pokémon uses a damaging move depends on its [level](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Level), its effective Attack or Special Attack [stat](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Statistic), the opponent's effective Defense or Special Defense stat, and the move's effective [power](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Power). In addition, various factors of damage modification may also affect the damage dealt.

More precisely, damage is calculated in each [Generation](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Generation) as:

### Generation I

Damage=((2×Level×Critical5+2)×Power×A/D50+2)×STAB×Type1×Type2×random

where:

- 5 References
- Level is the [level](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Level) of the attacking Pokémon.
- Critical is 2 for a [critical hit](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Critical_hit), and 1 otherwise.
- A is the effective Attack stat of the attacking Pokémon if the used move is a [physical move](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Physical_move), or the effective Special stat of the attacking Pokémon if the used move is a [special move](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Special_move)(for a [critical hit](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Critical_hit), all modifiers are ignored, and the unmodified Attack or Special is used instead). If either this or D are greater than 255, both are divided by 4 and rounded down.
- D is the effective Defense stat of the target if the used move is a [physical move](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Physical_move), or the effective Special stat of the target if the used move is an other [special move](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Special_move)(for a [critical hit](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Critical_hit), all modifiers are ignored, and the unmodified Defense or Special is used instead). If the move is physical and the target has [Reflect](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Reflect_(move)) up, or if the move is special and the target has [Light Screen](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Light_Screen_(move)) up, this value is doubled (unless it is a critical hit). If the move is [Explosion](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Explosion_(move)) or [Selfdestruct](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Self-Destruct_(move)), this value is halved (rounded down, with a minimum of 1). If either this or A are greater than 255, both are divided by 4 and rounded down. Unlike future Generations, if this is 0, the division is not made equal to 0; rather, the game will try to [divide by 0](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/List_of_glitches_in_Generation_I#Division_by_0) and softlock, hanging indefinitely until it is turned off.
- Power is the [power](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Power) of the used move.
- STAB is the [same-type attack bonus](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Same-type_attack_bonus). This is equal to 1.5 if the move's [type](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Type) matches any of the user's types, and 1 if otherwise. Internally, it is recognized as an addition of the damage calculated thus far divided by 2, rounded down, then added to the damage calculated thus far.
- Type1 is the [type effectiveness](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Type_effectiveness) of the used move against the target's type that comes first in the type matchup table, or only type if it only has one type. This can be 0.5 (not very effective), 1 (normally effective), 2 (super effective).
- Type2 is the [type effectiveness](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Type_effectiveness) of the used move against the target's type that comes second in the type matchup table. This can be 0.5 (not very effective), 1 (normally effective), 2 (super effective). If the target only has one type, Type2 is 1. If this would result in 0 damage, the calculation ends here and the move is stated to have missed, even if it would've hit.
- random is realized as a multiplication by a random uniformly distributed integer between 217 and 255 (inclusive), followed by an integer division by 255. If the calculated damage thus far is 1, random is always 1.

### Generation II

Damage=(((2×Level5+2)×Power×A/D50)×Item×Critical+2)×TK×Weather×Badge×STAB×Type×MoveMod×random×DoubleDmg

where:

MoveMod can be (and if the used move is not any of these, MoveMod is 1):

- If [Rollout](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Rollout_(move)) is used, 2(n+d), where n is the amount of successful and consecutive hits of the move, up to 4 (for the fifth hit), and d is 1 if [Defense Curl](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Defense_Curl_(move)) was used beforehand and 0 otherwise.
- If [Fury Cutter](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Fury_Cutter_(move)) is used, 2n, where n is the number of successful and consecutive uses of the move, up to 4.
- If [Rage](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Rage_(move)) is used, an integer value corresponding to the Rage counter, i.e. the number of times the user of Rage has been damaged by an attack while using Rage.

### Generation III

Damage=(((2×Level5+2)×Power×A/D50)×Burn×Screen×Targets×Weather×FF+2)×Stockpile×Critical×DoubleDmg×Charge×HH×STAB×Type1×Type2×random

where:

DoubleDmg is 2 if the used move is (and 1 if the used move is not any of these moves):

- [Gust](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Gust_(move)) or [Twister](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Twister_(move)) and the target is in the semi-invulnerable turn of [Fly](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Fly_(move)) or [Bounce](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Bounce_(move)).
- [Stomp](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Stomp_(move)), [Needle Arm](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Needle_Arm_(move)), [Astonish](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Astonish_(move)), or [Extrasensory](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Extrasensory_(move)) and the target has previously used [Minimize](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Minimize_(move)).
- [Surf](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Surf_(move)) or [Whirlpool](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Whirlpool_(move)) and the target is in the semi-invulnerable turn of [Dive](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Dive_(move)).
- [Earthquake](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Earthquake_(move)) or [Magnitude](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Magnitude_(move)) and the target is in the semi-invulnerable turn of [Dig](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Dig_(move)).
- [Pursuit](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Pursuit_(move)) and the target is attempting to [switch out](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Recall).
- [Facade](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Facade_(move)) and the user is [poisoned](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Poison_(status_condition)), [burned](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Burn_(status_condition)), or [paralyzed](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Paralysis_(status_condition)).
- [SmellingSalt](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/SmellingSalt_(move)) and the target is [paralyzed](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Paralysis_(status_condition)).
- [Revenge](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Revenge_(move)) and the attacker has been damaged by the target this turn.
- [Weather Ball](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Weather_Ball_(move)), there is non-clear [weather](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Weather), and no Pokémon on the field have the Ability [Cloud Nine](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Cloud_Nine_(Ability)) or [Air Lock](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Air_Lock_(Ability)).

### Generation IV

Damage=(((2×Level5+2)×Power×A/D50)×Burn×Screen×Targets×Weather×FF+2)×Critical×Item×First×random×STAB×Type1×Type2×SRF×EB×TL×Berry

where:

- random is realized as a multiplication by a random uniformly distributed integer between 85 and 100 (inclusive), followed by an integer division by 100. random is always 1 if [Spit Up](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Spit_Up_(move)) is used.
- Level is the [level](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Level) of the attacking Pokémon. If the used move is [Beat Up](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Beat_Up_(move)), L is instead the level of the Pokémon performing the strike.
- A is the effective Attack stat of the attacking Pokémon if the used move is a [physical move](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Physical_move), or the effective Special Attack stat of the attacking Pokémon if the used move is a [special move](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Special_move)(for a [critical hit](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Critical_hit), negative Attack or Special Attack stat stages are ignored). If the used move is [Beat Up](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Beat_Up_(move)), A is instead the base Attack of the Pokémon performing the strike.
- D is the effective Defense stat of the target if the used move is a [physical move](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Physical_move), or the effective Special Defense stat of the target if the used move is a [special move](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Special_move)(for a [critical hit](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Critical_hit), positive Defense or Special Defense stat stages are ignored). If the used move is [Beat Up](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Beat_Up_(move)), D is instead the base Defense of the target.
- Power is the effective [power](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Power) of the used move.
- Burn is 0.5 if the attacker is [burned](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Burn_(status_condition)), its Ability is not [Guts](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Guts_(Ability)), and the used move is a [physical move](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Physical_move), and 1 otherwise.
- Screen is 0.5 if the used move is physical and [Reflect](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Reflect_(move)) is present on the target's side of the field, or special and [Light Screen](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Light_Screen_(move)) is present. For a [Double Battle](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Double_Battle), Screen is instead 2/3; however, if in a Double Battle when the move is executed, the only Pokémon on the target's side of the field is the target (for moves with only one target), or there is only one target when the move is executed (for moves with more than one target), Screen remains as 0.5. Screen is 1 otherwise or if the used move lands a [critical hit](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Critical_hit).
- Targets is 0.75 in [Double Battles](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Double_Battle) if the used move [has more than](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Category:Moves_that_target_all_adjacent_foes) [one target](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Category:Moves_that_target_all_adjacent_Pok%C3%A9mon)(provided there is more than one such target when the move is executed, regardless of whether the move actually hits or can hit all the targets), and 1 otherwise.
- Weather is 1.5 if a [Water-type](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Water_(type)) move is being used during [rain](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Rain) or a [Fire-type](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Fire_(type)) move during [harsh sunlight](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Harsh_sunlight), and 0.5 if a Water-type move is used during harsh sunlight or a Fire-type move during rain, or [SolarBeam](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/SolarBeam_(move)) during any non-clear weather besides harsh sunlight, and 1 otherwise or if any Pokémon on the field have the Ability [Cloud Nine](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Cloud_Nine_(Ability)) or [Air Lock](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Air_Lock_(Ability)).
- FF is 1.5 if the used move is [Fire-type](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Fire_(type)), and the attacker's Ability is [Flash Fire](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Flash_Fire_(Ability)) that has been activated by a Fire-type move, and 1 otherwise.
- Critical is 2 for a [critical hit](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Critical_hit), 3 if the move lands a critical hit and the attacker's Ability is [Sniper](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Sniper_(Ability)), and 1 otherwise. It is always 1 if [Future Sight](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Future_Sight_(move)) or [Doom Desire](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Doom_Desire_(move)) is used, the target's Ability is [Battle Armor](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Battle_Armor_(Ability)) or [Shell Armor](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Shell_Armor_(Ability)), the target is under the effect of [Lucky Chant](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Lucky_Chant_(move)), or if the battle is the first one against [Starly](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Starly_(Pok%C3%A9mon)) [DP](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Pok%C3%A9mon_Diamond_and_Pearl_Versions).
- Item is 1.3 if the attacker is holding a [Life Orb](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Life_Orb), 1+n10 if the attacker is holding a [Metronome](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Metronome_(item)), where n is the amount of times the same move has been successfully and consecutively used, up to 10, and 1 otherwise.
- First is 1.5 if the used move was stolen with [Me First](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Me_First).
- random is realized a random integer from 85 to 100, inclusive, divided by 100. random is always 1 if [Spit Up](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Spit_Up_(move)) is used.
- STAB is the [same-type attack bonus](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Same-type_attack_bonus). This is equal to 1.5 if the move's [type](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Type) matches any of the user's types, 2 if the user of the move additionally has [Adaptability](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Adaptability_(Ability)), and 1 if otherwise.
- Type1 is the [type effectiveness](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Type_effectiveness) of the used move against the target's first type (or only type, if it only has a single type). This can be 0.5 (not very effective), 1 (normally effective), or 2 (super effective). If the used move is [Struggle](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Struggle_(move)), [Future Sight](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Future_Sight_(move)), [Beat Up](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Beat_Up_(move)), or [Doom Desire](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Doom_Desire_(move)), both Type1 and Type2 are always 1.
- Type2 is the [type effectiveness](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Type_effectiveness) of the used move against the target's second type. This can be 0.5 (not very effective), 1 (normally effective), or 2 (super effective). If the target only has a single type, Type2 is 1.
- SRF is 0.75 if the used move is super effective, the target's Ability is [Solid Rock](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Solid_Rock_(Ability)) or [Filter](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Filter_(Ability)), and the attacker's Ability is not [Mold Breaker](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Mold_Breaker_(Ability)), and 1 otherwise.
- EB is 1.2 if the used move is super effective and the attacker is holding an [Expert Belt](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Expert_Belt), and 1 otherwise.
- TL is 2 if the used move is not very effective and the attacker's Ability is [Tinted Lens](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Tinted_Lens_(Ability)), and 1 otherwise.
- Berry is 0.5 if the used move is super effective and the target is holding the [Berry that weakens it](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Category:Damage-reducing_Berries), or [Normal-type](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Normal_(type)) and the target is holding a [Chilan Berry](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Chilan_Berry), and 1 otherwise.

### Generation V onward

Unless otherwise specified, all divisions and multiplications past the initial base damage calculation are rounded to the nearest integer if the result is not an integer (rounding down at 0.5).

Damage=((2×Level5+2)×Power×A/D50+2)×Targets×PB×Weather×GlaiveRush×Critical×random×STAB×Type×Burn×other×ZMove×TeraShield

where

Critical is 1.5 (2 in [Generation V](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Generation_V)) for a [critical hit](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Critical_hit), and 1 otherwise. Decimals are rounded down to the nearest integer. It is always 1 if the target's Ability is [Battle Armor](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Battle_Armor_(Ability)) or [Shell Armor](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Shell_Armor_(Ability)) or if the target is under the effect of [Lucky Chant](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Lucky_Chant_(move)).

- Conversely, unless critical hits are prevented entirely by one of the above effects, Critical will always be 1.5 (or 2 in Generation V) if the used move is [Storm Throw](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Storm_Throw_(move)), [Frost Breath](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Frost_Breath_(move)), [Zippy Zap](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Zippy_Zap_(move)), [Surging Strikes](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Surging_Strikes_(move)), [Wicked Blow](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Wicked_Blow_(move)), or [Flower Trick](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Flower_Trick_(move)), the target is [poisoned](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Poison_(status_condition)) and the attacker's Ability is [Merciless](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Merciless_(Ability)), or if the user is under the effect of [Laser Focus](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Laser_Focus_(move)).

random is a random factor. Namely, it is recognized as a multiplication from a random integer between 85 and 100, inclusive, then divided by 100.

- If the battle is taking place as a [Pokéstar Studios](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Pok%C3%A9star_Studios) film, random is always 1.

STAB is the [same-type attack bonus](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Same-type_attack_bonus). This is equal to 1.5 if the move's [type](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Type) matches any of the user's types, 2 if the user of the move additionally has [Adaptability](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Adaptability_(Ability)), and 1 otherwise or if the attacker and/or used move is [typeless](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Typeless). If the used move is a combination Pledge move, STAB is always 1.5 (or 2 if the user's Ability is Adaptability). When [Terastallized](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Terastal_phenomenon), STAB is (if not 1):

2 if the move's type matches the same Tera Type as one of the Pokemon's original types and the attacker's Ability is not Adaptability, or the situation above, if the attacker's Ability is Adaptability.

- However, if STAB only applies from the attacker's original type(s), not its Tera Type, STAB will always be 1.5, even if the attacker's Ability is Adaptability.

Type is the [type effectiveness](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Type#Type_effectiveness). This can be 0.125, 0.25, 0.5 (not very effective); 1 (normally effective); 2, 4, or 8 (super effective), depending on both the move's and target's [types](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Type). The 0.125 and 8 can potentially be obtained on a Pokémon under the effect of [Forest's Curse](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Forest%27s_Curse_(move)) or [Trick-or-Treat](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Trick-or-Treat_(move)). If the used move is [Struggle](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Struggle_(move)) or [typeless](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Typeless) [Revelation Dance](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Revelation_Dance_(move)), or the target is typeless, Type is always 1. Decimals are rounded down to the nearest integer. Certain effects can modify this, namely:

- If the target is an [ungrounded](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Grounded) [Flying-type](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Flying_(type)) that is not being grounded by any other effect and is holding an [Iron Ball](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Iron_Ball) or under the effect of [Thousand Arrows](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Thousand_Arrows_(move)), Type is equal to 1.
- If the target is a [grounded](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Grounded) [Flying-type](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Flying_(type))(unless grounded by an Iron Ball or Thousand Arrows, as above), treat [Ground](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Ground_(type))'s matchup against Flying as 1.
- If the target is holding a [Ring Target](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Ring_Target) and the used move is of a type it would otherwise be immune to, treat that particular type matchup as 1.
- If the attacker's Ability is [Scrappy](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Scrappy_(Ability)), treat [Normal](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Normal_(type)) and [Fighting](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Fighting_(type))'s type matchups against [Ghost](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Ghost_(type)) as 1.
- If the target is under the effect of [Foresight](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Foresight_(move)), [Odor Sleuth](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Odor_Sleuth_(move)) or [Miracle Eye](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Miracle_Eye_(move)), and the target is of a type that would otherwise grant immunity to the used move, treat that particular type matchup as 1.
- If the used move is [Freeze-Dry](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Freeze-Dry_(move)), treat the move's type's matchup against [Water](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Water_(type)) as 2.
- If the used move is [Flying Press](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Flying_Press_(move)), consider both the move's type effectiveness and the [Flying](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Flying_(type)) type's against the target, and multiply them together.
- If [strong winds](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Strong_winds) are in effect and the used move would be super effective against [Flying](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Flying_(type)), treat the type matchup against Flying as 1 instead.
- If the target is under the effect of [Tar Shot](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Tar_Shot_(move)) and the used move is [Fire-type](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Fire_(type)), multiply Type by 2.

If multiple effects influence the other value, their values stack multiplicatively, in the order listed above. This is done by starting at 4096, multiplying it by each number above in the order listed above, and rounding to the nearest integer whenever the result is not an integer (rounding up at 0.5). When the final value is obtained, it is divided by 4096, and this becomes other. For example, if both [Multiscale](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Multiscale_(Ability)) and a [Chilan Berry](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Chilan_Berry) take effect, other is 4096×0.5×0.54096 = 0.25.

- other is 1 in most cases, and a different multiplier when specific interactions of moves, Abilities, or items take effect, in this order (and if multiple moves, Abilities, or items take effect, they do so in the order of the out-of-battle [Speed](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Stat#Speed) stats of the Pokémon with them):
- ZMove is 0.25 if the move is a [Z-Move](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Z-Move) or [Max Move](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Max_Move) and the target would be [protected](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Protection) from that move (e.g. by [Protect](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Protect_(move))), and 1 otherwise. (If this multiplier is applied, a message is displayed that the target "couldn't fully protect" itself.)
- TeraShield is applied in [Tera Raid Battles](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Tera_Raid_Battle) when the Raid boss's shield is active. It is 0.2 if the player's Pokémon is not [Terastallized](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Terastal_phenomenon), 0.35 if it is but the used move is not of its Tera Type, and 0.75 if it is and the used move is of its Tera Type. The result is subject to standard rounding (rounding up at 0.5).

In the first four generations, during the calculation, all operations are carried out on integers internally—this means that all division operations are [truncated integer division](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rounding#Rounding_to_integer)(i.e. rounding down if the result is not an integer), and the results of multiplication operations are rounded down afterwards (truncating any fractional part). From [Generation V](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Generation_V) onward, there are three different types of rounding; a [flooring](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Floor_(function))(the same as previous generations), rounding to the nearest integer while rounding down at 0.5, and rounding to the nearest integer while rounding up at 0.5.

If the calculation yields 0, the move will deal 1 HP damage instead (unless Type is equal to 0, in which case damage calculation is skipped entirely); however, in Generation V, a move may deal 0 damage when other is less than 1, because the routine to prevent 0 damage is erroneously performed before applying the other factor.

### Example

Imagine a [level](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Level) 75 [Glaceon](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Glaceon_(Pok%C3%A9mon)) that does not suffer a [burn](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Burn_(status_condition)) and holds no [item](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Item) with an effective Attack stat of 123 uses [Ice Fang](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Ice_Fang_(move))(an [Ice-type](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Ice_(type)) [physical move](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Physical_move) with a [power](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Power) of 65) against a [Garchomp](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Garchomp_(Pok%C3%A9mon)) with an effective Defense stat of 163 in [Generation VI](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Generation_VI), and does not land a [critical hit](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Critical_hit). Then, the move will receive [STAB](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Same-type_attack_bonus), because Glaceon's Ice type matches the move's: STAB = 1.5. Additionally, Garchomp is [Dragon](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Dragon_(type))/ [Ground](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Ground_(type)), and therefore has a double weakness to the move's Ice type: Type = 4. All other (non-random) modifiers will be 1. This effectively gives

Damage=((2×755+2)×65×123/16350+2)×1×1×1×1×(rand∈[0.85,1.00])×1.5×4×1×1=rand∈[168,196]

That means Ice Fang will do between 168 and 196 HP damage, depending on luck.

If the same Glaceon holds a [Muscle Band](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Muscle_Band) and its Ice Fang lands a [critical hit](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Critical_hit) against Garchomp, Ice Fang's effective power will be boosted by the Muscle Band by (approximately) 10% to become 71, and it will also be Critical = 1.5:

Damage=((2×755+2)×71×123/16350+2)×1×1×1×1.5×(rand∈[0.85,1.00])×1.5×4×1×1=rand∈[268,324]

That means Ice Fang will now do between 268 and 324 HP damage, depending on luck.

### Pokémon Legends: Arceus

In [Pokémon Legends: Arceus](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Pok%C3%A9mon_Legends:_Arceus), a new damage calculation method[2] is used:

All multiplications and divisions are rounded down to the nearest integer unless specified.

Damage=(((100+A+(15×Level))×Power)/(D+50)5)×(AtkMod×DefMod)×random×Type×OtherMods OtherMods=Weather×Critical×STAB×BurnFrostbite×Drowsy×FixatedOffense×FixatedDefense×Primed

where

A is the effective Attack stat of the attacking Pokémon if the used move is a [physical move](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Physical_move), or the effective Special Attack stat of the attacking Pokémon if the used move is a [special move](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Special_move).

- If the attacker is under [Slow Start](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Slow_Start_(Ability)), then the physical Attack stat will be halved at this step of the calculation.
- If the attacker is filled with Wild Might or Terrible Might, both Attack stats will be multiplied by 1.5.

D is the effective Defense stat of the target if the used move is a [physical move](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Physical_move), or the effective Special Defense of the target if the used move is an other [special move](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Special_move).

- If the target is filled with Wild Might or Terrible Might, both Defense stats will be multiplied by 1.5.

random is realized as a multiplication by a random integer between 85 and 100 (inclusive), and divided by 100.

- Splinter damage (from [Ceaseless Edge](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Ceaseless_Edge_(move)), [Pin Missile](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Pin_Missile_(move)), [Spikes](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Spikes_(move)), [Stealth Rock](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Stealth_Rock_(move)) and [Stone Axe](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Stone_Axe_(move))) is not subject to this factor.

### Pokémon Legends: Z-A

In [Pokémon Legends: Z-A](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Pok%C3%A9mon_Legends:_Z-A), a damage calculation method similar to the one used from [Generation V](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Generation_V) onwards is used, though it does feature its own share of differences from it:[3]

All multiplications and divisions are rounded down to the nearest integer unless specified.

Damage=(A×Power×(2×Level5+2)50×D+2)×Weather×Critical×random×STAB×Type×Burn×other×Protect×0.7

where

A is the effective Attack stat of the attacking Pokémon if the used move is a [physical move](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Physical_move), or the effective Special Attack stat of the attacking Pokémon if the used move is a [special move](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Special_move)(ignoring stat drops for a [critical hit](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Critical_hit)).

- It is multiplied by ×1.5 if the user's stat is boosted, or ×0.67 if the stat is dropped.
- It is multiplied by ×2 if the user's Trainer has a red [X Attack](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/X_Attack) buff in the [Z-A Battle Club](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Z-A_Battle_Club).
- It is multiplied by ×2 if the user is a [wild](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Wild_Pok%C3%A9mon) [alpha Pokémon](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Alpha_Pok%C3%A9mon)
- It can be multiplied by ×1.1, ×1.25, or ×1.5 if Levels 1, 2, or 3, respectively, of [Attack Power](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Donut#Flavor_Power) or Sp. Atk Power is active.

D is the effective Defense stat of the target if the used move is a [physical move](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Physical_move) or a [special move that uses the target's Defense stat](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Category:Moves_that_use_stats_from_different_categories), or the effective Special Defense of the target if the used move is an other [special move](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Special_move)(ignoring a stat boost for a [critical hit](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Critical_hit)).

- It is multiplied by ×1.5 if the target's stat is boosted, or ×0.67 if the stat is dropped.
- It is multiplied by ×2 if the target's Trainer has a blue [X Defense](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/X_Defense) buff in the [Z-A Battle Club](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Z-A_Battle_Club).
- It is multiplied by ×2 if the target is a [wild](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Wild_Pok%C3%A9mon) [alpha Pokémon](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Alpha_Pok%C3%A9mon)
- It can be multiplied by ×1.1, ×1.25, or ×1.5 if Levels 1, 2, or 3, respectively, of [Defense Power](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Donut#Flavor_Power) or Sp. Def Power is active.
- It can be multiplied by ×10.9, ×10.8, or ×10.65 if Levels 1, 2, or 3, respectively, of [Resistance Power](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Donut#Flavor_Power) is active and the type of the used move matches the type Resistance Power guards against.

Power is the base [power](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Power) of the used move.

- It can be multiplied by ×1.1, ×1.25, or ×1.5 if Levels 1, 2, or 3, respectively, of [Move Power](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Donut#Flavor_Power) is active and the type of the used move matches the type Move Power boosts.

| Effectiveness | Standard | Boss |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Base | Plus Move | Base | Plus Move |
| Super effective | 8× | 10.4× | 1.512× | 6.552× |
| 4× | 5.2× | 0.756× | 3.276× |
| 2× | 2.6× | 0.6× | 2.6× |
| Normally effective | 1× | 1.2× | 0.3× | 1.2× |
| Not very effective | 0.6× | 0.72× | 0.18× | 0.72× |
| 0.3× | 0.36× | 0.09× | 0.36× |
| 0.15× | 0.18× | 0.045× | 0.18× |

- Type is the [type effectiveness](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Type#Type_effectiveness). The multiplier can be affected depending on if a [Plus Move](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Plus_Move) is used and if used against a [Rogue Mega Evolution](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Rogue_Mega_Evolution) or equivalent boss battle. If a Plus Move is used against in a boss battle, the Plus Move multiplier is used.
- Against a boss, 0.3× is applied to non-Plus Moves, and 0.63× is applied to super effective moves with a 4× or higher type multiplier.
- The 0.15 and 8 (as well as the 0.18 and 10.4) can potentially be obtained on a Pokémon under the effect of [Forest's Curse](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Forest%27s_Curse_(move)) or [Trick-or-Treat](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Trick-or-Treat_(move)).
- If the target is typeless, Type is always 1; in Legends: Z-A, this is only used against the [Hyperrogue Ange Floette](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Hyperrogue_Ange_Floette) flowers, which are considered to be typeless.
- If the attacker is a boss, and the target would otherwise be immune to the attack, Type is 0.3.
- If the target is an [ungrounded](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Grounded) [Flying-type](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Flying_(type)) that is not being grounded by any other effect and is under the effect of [Thousand Arrows](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Thousand_Arrows_(move)), Type is equal to 1.
- If the target is a [grounded](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Grounded) [Flying-type](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Flying_(type))(unless grounded by an Thousand Arrows, as above), treat [Ground](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Ground_(type))'s matchup against Flying as 1.
- If the used move is [Freeze-Dry](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Freeze-Dry_(move)), treat the move's type's matchup against [Water](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Water_(type)) as 2.
- If the used move is [Flying Press](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Flying_Press_(move)), consider both the move's type effectiveness and the [Flying](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Flying_(type)) type's against the target, and multiply them together.

- Burn is 0.5 if the attacker is [burned](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Burn_(status_condition)) and the used move is a [physical move](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Physical_move), and 1 otherwise.
- other is 1 in most cases, and a different multiplier when specific interactions of moves or items take effect, in this order:

| Effect | Value | Detail |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [Reflect](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Reflect_(move)) and [Light Screen](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Light_Screen_(move)) | 0.66 | If in effect on the target's side, the used move is physical (Reflect) or special (Light Screen), and the move is not a [critical hit](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Critical_hit). |
| [Type-resist Berries](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Category:Damage-reducing_Berries) | 0.5 | If held by the target, the move is of the corresponding type, and is super effective (Type > 1); for the Chilan Berry, the used move must simply only be [Normal-type](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Normal_(type)) |
| [Expert Belt](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Expert_Belt) | 1.2 | If held by the attacker and the move is super effective (Type > 1) |
| [Life Orb](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Life_Orb) | 1.3 | If held by the attacker |

If multiple effects influence the other value, their values stack multiplicatively, in the order listed above. This is done by starting with 1, multiplying the multipliers together, multiplying the final multiplier by the result thus far, and rounding down the result to the nearest integer.

- Protect is 0.25 if the move is a [Plus Move](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Plus_Move) and the target would be [protected](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Protection) from that move (e.g. by [Protect](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Protect_(move))), and 1 if no protection is used. (If this multiplier is applied, a message is displayed that the target "couldn't fully protect" itself.)

If the calculation yields 0, the move will deal 1 HP damage instead (unless Type is equal to 0, in which case damage calculation is skipped entirely).

If the move would deal over 65535 damage, it will instead be capped at 65535; notably, this is different from in [Generation V](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Generation_V) to [Pokémon Scarlet and Violet](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Pok%C3%A9mon_Scarlet_and_Violet), where the damage instead would roll over to 0 at 65536.

### Pokémon GO

In [Pokémon GO](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Pok%C3%A9mon_GO), damage is calculated differently due to different variables existing in the game.

Damage=Mushroom×(⌊0.5×Power×AttackDefense×Modifier⌋+1)

where

For [Shadow Pokémon](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Shadow_Pok%C3%A9mon_(GO)):

- ×65 is applied to Attack
- ×56 is applied to Defense

and

Modifier=Type×STAB×Weather×Friendship×Dodged×Mega×Trainer×Charge×Party×Support×Spread×BehemothBlade×BehemothBash×Shield

where

The following variables are applied in [Gym](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Gym_(GO)), [Raid Battles](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Raid_Battle_(GO)), and [Max Battles](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Max_Battle) only, and are 1 otherwise.

Friendship is applied when battling with [Friends](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Friends_(GO)) and varies depending on the Friendship level.

- 1.03 if Good Friends
- 1.05 if Great Friends
- 1.07 if Ultra Friends
- 1.1 if Best Friends
- 1.12 if Forever Friends
- 1 otherwise

Dodged is 0.25 if the attack was successfully dodged, and 1 if otherwise.

- Gym defenders and Raid Bosses will never dodge a player's attacks

Mega is greater than 1 when there is one or more [Mega-Evolved](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Mega_Evolution) Pokémon on the battlefield.

- 1.1 if none of the Mega-Evolved Pokémon have the same type as the move
- 1.3 if one or more Mega-Evolved Pokémon have the same type as the move

BehemothBash is applied when the defender has the [Behemoth Bash](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Behemoth_Bash_(move)) [adventure effect](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Adventure_Effect) and varies depending on the battlefield used

- 0.9090909091 in [Raid Battles](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Raid_Battle_(GO))
- 0.9523809524 in [Max Battles](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Max_Battle)

BehemothBlade is applied when the attacker has the [Behemoth Blade](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Behemoth_Blade_(move)) [adventure effect](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Adventure_Effect) and varies depending on the battlefield used

- 1.1 in [Raid Battles](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Raid_Battle_(GO))
- 1.05 in [Max Battles](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Max_Battle)

The following variables are applied in [Trainer Battles](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Trainer_Battle_(GO)) only, and are 1 otherwise.

Charge is applied only for Charged Attacks, and its value depends on the player's score during the minigame. The possible ranges are

- Charge=1 if "Excellent!"
- Charge∈[0.75,1) if "Great!"
- Charge∈[0.5,0.75) if "Nice!"
- Charge∈[0.25,0.5) otherwise

The following variable is applied in [Max Battles](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Max_Battle) only, and are 1 otherwise.

Support is applied when there are Pokémon placed at the [Power Spot](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Power_Spot_(GO)).

- 1.1 if 1 Pokémon placed (1 support icon)
- 1.15 if 2-3 Pokémon placed (2 support icons)
- 1.188 if 4-14 Pokémon placed (3 support icons)
- 1.2 if 15 or more Pokémon placed (4 support icons)
- 1 otherwise

By the damage formula, moves that have a power of 0 such as [Splash](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Splash_(move)) and [Yawn](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Yawn_(move)) and moves shielded by [Protect Shields](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Protect_Shield) will always do 1 HP damage.
