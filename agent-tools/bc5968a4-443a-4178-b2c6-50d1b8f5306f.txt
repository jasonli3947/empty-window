## How to Use the Damage Calculator

Click Select or Load for the Attacker and Defender to choose the Pokemon.

---

Add up to 4 moves for the Attacker.

---

You can swap between the Attacker and Defender by clicking the Swap Icon , and their moves and other data will be retained when you swap back!

Update the Stats for the Attacker and Defender.

---

For the Nature Modifier, select 1.1x if the stat has a Beneficial Nature, or 0.9x if the stat has a Hindering Nature.

---

Update the Ability, Held Item, and other Conditions for the Attacker and Defender.

---

Some Abilities like Huge Power will update the calculations when set.

---

If needed, update the Screen and Recurring Damage for the Defender.

---

Reference the Speed Comparison to see which Pokemon will move first.

---

Check your finalized Damage Result.

---

The green section of the Damage Result shows the remaining HP, while the empty (gray) section shows how much damage has been dealt.

---

Click To Add to stack multiple attacks.

---

Copy the Share link to save and send your results.
